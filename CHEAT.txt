Cheat mode is toggled by:
Pressing <Shift> three times.


Kill off Player by:
Pressing <Delete>.


Toggle Player Immunity by:
Pressing <Period>.


Select object to toggle spawning by:
Pressing <Comma>.

NOTE: Duck spawning can not be disabled.

> Stop spawning all objects by:
> Pressing <Comma>.

> Stop spawning all enemies by:
> Pressing <E>.

> Stop spawning Zombies by:
> Pressing <Z>.
